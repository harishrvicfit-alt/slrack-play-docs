Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Catch The Sun - SL Rack collects, uses and protects personal data when you use the mobile game and its connected online services.

Last updated: April 15, 2026
Android package name: com.haris.catch_the_sun_sl_rack

1. Who we are

Catch The Sun - SL Rack is a branded promotional mobile game operated for SL Rack.

Contact: Haris Hrvic - harishrvicfit@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

  • email address
  • display name / player name
  • country
  • newsletter opt-in preference
  • contest rules acceptance status
  • email verification status
  • score submissions and ranking data
  • technical submission data such as install ID and game version

3. How we use data

  • to create and manage player profiles
  • to verify users by email OTP
  • to connect scores to online accounts
  • to display monthly and all-time leaderboards
  • to manage prize-related and competition-related flows
  • to apply basic fraud prevention and anti-cheat validation
  • to send newsletter communication only if you opted in

4. Third-party services

This app uses third-party services and infrastructure, including Supabase and Google Play.

5. Leaderboards

If you submit scores, your player name, country, score and ranking may be displayed within leaderboard or competition screens in the app.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction or deletion of your personal data.

Contact: Haris Hrvic - harishrvicfit@gmail.com

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version published at this URL is the current version.
